Our Story

We are a small Arizona company started in 2022. This business idea was prompted by an event that took place in the early summer of 2022. Specifically, a climbing friend, who is himself a semi-professional drone pilot, contacted me to help him retrieve two drones that had crashed landed on a pinnacle along the Apache trail. A professional drone pilot was attempting to retrieve one drone with another when it became snagged on a bush. Both drones were inaccessible to the pilot; technical rock-climbing skills were required to recover the drones.

As a rock climber and route developer Stan knew that I had the necessary skills and gear to climb the pinnacle to retrieve the drones. Using some climbing hardware and rock-climbing gear we were able to ascend the pinnacle and recover the drones. We rappelled to the ground, hiked back to the trail head, and returned the drones to the owner. Thus, Drone Recovery Services (DRS) was born. A video of the recovery is depicted on the home page of this website when it loads.

Our Services

We retrieve drones that have been inadvertently lost by, or otherwise inaccessible to, a drone pilot. Our fees are based on a percentage of the replacement cost of the drone. At present we charge 10 percent of the drone retail value. Prices may be negotiated under special circumstances.

Our Experience

Although DRS is a new company, I bring 30 years of technical rock-climbing experience to the company. I recently retired from Arizona State University as a research scientist. As a scientist I am constantly analyzing information and creating solutions. This experience, along with my problem-solving skills, permits me to analyze each particular situation to create a successful recovery plan for lost drones. It would be expected that each particular situation brings different climbing and rigging challenges. This service thus requires multiple skills and knowledge that we provide.
